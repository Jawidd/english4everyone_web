## Why volunteer?

The need to bring communities closer together was demonstrated by the horrific riots of summer 2024.

You can help bring people together in Leeds by volunteering with English4All.

---

## Benefits

- Volunteering always looks great on a CV
- Written References provided to those looking for work/education
- Gain teaching experience
- Develop other skills and confidence
- Build a social network
- Attend social activities
- Special discounts and exclusive invitations to events held by our sister project [The Arches Social](/arc-social)

---

## Vacancies

**Volunteer Teachers** (requires a teaching certificate of some kind, preferably CELTA/TESOL)

Other vacancies not requiring qualifications or experience:

- Classroom Assistants
- Administrators in HR, Accounts, Marketing, Governance and more
- Social Media content creators
- Volunteers for [The Arches Social](/arc-social)

Days/times to volunteer are left up to the volunteers to choose.
