## Why volunteer?

Volunteering is a great way to:

- Help your community
- Share your skills
- Meet new people
- Make a difference

---

## Teaching roles

We need English teachers. You do not need a formal teaching qualification. We will train you.

You will:

- Teach a Saturday class (2 hours)
- Help students with reading, writing, and speaking

---

## Admin and support roles

We also need help with:

- Organising classes
- Welcoming students
- Social media and communications
- Fundraising
