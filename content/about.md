## Who we are

English4All Leeds is a volunteer-led charity. We help adults learn English.

We believe everyone deserves the chance to learn English. It helps people:

- Talk to their neighbours
- Find work
- Feel confident in daily life

## Our mission

We want to help people learn English and build confidence.

We welcome everyone. All levels. All backgrounds.

## Our volunteers

Our teachers are volunteers. They give their time to help others.

We are always looking for new volunteers. [Find out how to help](/volunteering).
