## Who we are

English4All in Leeds is a volunteer-led charity providing free ESOL English classes to adult migrants, together with organising social activities to help bring our community together.

Through our project, beneficiaries will:

- Build confidence and develop
- Through improved English gain opportunities to access employment, education or training
- Make friends and reduce isolation

## Our Mission

English4All in Leeds's mission statement is "To promote social cohesion in Leeds through shared learning, socialising and networking".

PM Keir Starmer once described a threat that the UK was turning into "an island of strangers". This is what English4All is battling against.

## Our Volunteers

All our volunteer teachers are qualified and experienced, with more than 80% holding either CELTA or TESOL teaching certificates. Many have themselves learnt English as a foreign language, giving them lived experience of what it's like for migrants.
